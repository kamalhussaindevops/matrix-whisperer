The instinct when comparing two charts is to rank them — whose energy is stronger, whose number is higher, whose karmic load is lighter. That framing misses the point. The matrix comparison process is not a ranking system. It is a pattern-interaction map. The question is never "who wins?" It is "how do these two energetic blueprints interact, and what does that interaction require from both people to function well?"
